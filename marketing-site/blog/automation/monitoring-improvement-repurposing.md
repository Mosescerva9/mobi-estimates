# SEO Monitoring, Improvement, and Repurposing Plan

## Connectors required
Do not fabricate metrics. Use only connected sources:
- Google Search Console: indexation, sitemap discovery, impressions, clicks, CTR, average position, queries, crawl errors.
- Approved analytics platform: article engagement, CTA clicks, internal-link clicks, scroll depth, qualified leads, assisted conversions.
- Site checks: broken links, Core Web Vitals / local performance, source expiration.

## Weekly report
- Newly published pages and live QA status.
- Indexation status and sitemap discovery.
- Top queries by impressions/clicks.
- Articles ranking positions 5–20.
- Broken links / crawl errors.
- CTA click-through and internal-link clicks when analytics is connected.
- Recommended next actions.

## Monthly report
- Cluster-level performance.
- New opportunity queue updates.
- Articles needing refresh.
- Content cannibalization warnings.
- Conversion-assisted article list.
- Repurposing backlog.

## Improvement triggers
- High impressions but low CTR.
- Ranking positions 5–20.
- Declining impressions.
- Outdated source or stale product statement.
- Broken link.
- Missing internal links.
- Content-cluster gap.
- Weak CTA conversion.
- Meaningful search-intent change.
- Article older than review interval.

## Allowed improvement actions
- Improve title/meta description.
- Add missing sections.
- Strengthen examples.
- Update sources.
- Add internal links.
- Improve CTA placement.
- Update visuals.
- Create supporting article.
- Merge overlapping content.
- Retire obsolete content.

High-risk factual changes require review.

## Repurposing workflow after live QA passes
Draft only unless a separate social/email automation policy is approved:
- LinkedIn post.
- Email newsletter blurb.
- YouTube community post.
- Short-form video hooks.
- Future YouTube script.
- Internal sales enablement note.
- Lead magnet proposal when appropriate.

Derivative assets must reference the final published URL and verified claims only.
