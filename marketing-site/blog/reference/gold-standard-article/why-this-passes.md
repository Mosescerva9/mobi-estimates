## Quality principles preserved
- Satisfies a clear informational search intent: contractors trying to understand markup vs. gross margin.
- Provides original utility beyond SERP summary: formulas, worked examples, a conversion table, a pricing-method comparison, decision checklist, and FAQ.

## Future rules derived from this reference
- Every article must have a documented primary query, intent, and audience.
- Every article must include topic-appropriate original utility, not surface-level summary.
- Every calculation must include base, formula, and checked result.
- Draft and production states must never be confused.
- No unpublished related post may become a live production link.
- All CTA links must be resolved as the browser will resolve them from the final URL.
- Low-risk publication still requires live QA and rollback readiness.
