# Mobi Estimates Repo Guidance

## SEO blog work
For any Mobi SEO blog post, resource article, YouTube-to-blog transformation, topic-cluster plan, article refresh, blog publication, or blog-performance improvement:

1. Load `skills/mobi-seo-blog/SKILL.md`.
2. Use `marketing-site/blog/reference/gold-standard-article/` as the quality benchmark.
3. Do not copy the gold-standard article’s wording, examples, title style, or exact layout.
4. Match or exceed its usefulness, evidence discipline, Mobi-claim restraint, responsive behavior, technical validation, and publication safety.
5. Convert meaningful Moses corrections into documentation, validators, tests, reusable components, or the post-approval learning log when they are durable/global.
6. Publish only through the controlled blog wrapper and explicit approval path; do not enable general autonomous publishing unless separately authorized.
