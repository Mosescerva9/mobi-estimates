# Mobi SEO Blog Definition of Done

An article is not ready to publish unless every applicable item is complete.

- [ ] Primary query and search intent are documented in canonical front matter.
- [ ] Cannibalization check passes against published posts, drafts, service pages, landing pages, YouTube-derived drafts, and Mobi resources.
- [ ] Content brief exists and names audience, intent, differentiation, risk, sources, CTA, and original utility.
- [ ] Sources are verified for freshness and attribution.
- [ ] At least two topic-appropriate original utility elements are present.
- [ ] Unsupported claims are absent.
- [ ] Mobi claims are checked against canonical docs.
- [ ] CTA is approved for the article risk/intent.
- [ ] Metadata is complete: title, SEO title, meta description, excerpt, slug, canonical, OG image, risk, status.
- [ ] Article schema is valid and matches visible content.
- [ ] Internal links resolve from the final URL.
- [ ] Unpublished related posts are not live links.
- [ ] Mobile and desktop previews pass at 320, 360, 390, 430, 768, 1024, and 1440 px when publication is being considered.
- [ ] Gold-standard comparison passes without rewarding surface similarity.
- [ ] Quality rubric overall score is at least 85 and no blocking category fails.
- [ ] Risk classification is complete.
- [ ] Required domain review is complete for medium/high-risk claims.
- [ ] Controlled publication dry run passes.
- [ ] Live QA and rollback point are ready before production publication.
