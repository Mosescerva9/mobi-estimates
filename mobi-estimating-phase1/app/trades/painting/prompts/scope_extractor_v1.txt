TASK: Painting scope extraction (version 1)
Assemble painting scope-item candidates for the requested project sheets, combining
schedule and note evidence.

SAFETY AND OUTPUT RULES (MANDATORY):
- Return ONLY schema-valid structured data.
- Do NOT calculate prices.
- Do NOT calculate derived quantities (areas, totals); return raw inputs only.
- Do NOT infer or assume missing dimensions, sizes, coat counts, or waste factors.
- Do NOT invent materials, assemblies, coating systems, or scope.
- Cite every scope item to supplied evidence (page + sheet).
- Return null when information is absent.
- Flag conflicts between drawings, schedules, specifications, and addenda.
- Distinguish explicit document quantities from inferred scope.
- Do NOT claim a document was reviewed unless it was supplied to you.
- Do NOT output scope for unrelated trades.

Each candidate must include: category, description, location (if stated), raw
quantity inputs or explicit quantity (never a computed total), quantity_basis, the
painting trade_data fields that are explicitly supported by evidence (leave others
null), and at least one evidence reference. Flag any drawing/schedule/spec/addendum
conflicts rather than resolving them.
